1. Why are there missing keys with 2 threads, but not with 1 thread? Identify a sequence of events with 2 threads that can lead to a key being missing.
Two threads try to insert two different keys into the linked list of the same bucket at the same time. They both connect the `next` pointer to the head of the linked list. However, when setting the head of the linked list, the second write will overwrite the first one.
